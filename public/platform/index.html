What We Solve

E-Waste & Critical Materials Recovery

Maximize value from complex waste streams. We design and optimize processes to recover critical materials and recycle high-value components from solar panels and e-waste.

Biofuel & Biowaste Valorization

Transform agricultural and industrial waste into high-value fuels and chemicals. Our platform rapidly optimizes conversion pathways for maximum yield and efficiency.

Green Ammonia & Sustainable Fertilizers

Revolutionize agriculture by decarbonizing one of the world's most energy-intensive industrial processes. Our platform designs novel catalysts that mimic nature, enabling the low-energy production of green ammonia for carbon-neutral fertilizers.
